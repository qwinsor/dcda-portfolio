Semester Reflection

What I learned: Lab 1: I learned the basics of adding pictures and files into VScode so that they can be used on my website. I also learned how to adjust colors, fonts, spacing, and simple things of that nature. Lab 2: It was nice to learn how to find the limits of different AI tools. I had fun messing around and tying to see how far I could push these programs. Lab 3: I learned how to evaluate visualizations of data. This includes physical asthetics as well as the information that is being portrayed.

Challenges I overcame: Lab 1 : It was a little difficult for me to remember all the steps for things like adding an image, but once I remembered them all the order didnt really matter. Also I didnt put the code in properly, so I had to look over all my work. Lab 2: Figuring out how to challenge these AI tools was the hardest part. I felt like I was forcing prompts that I knew wouldn't work just to scale it back a little bit for the next attempt. Lab 3: Other than finding a visualization to evaluate it was a pretty straight forward lab.

How I grew: Lab 1: I feel a lot more comfortable with the interfaces and softwares. I feel a lot more confident going in to lab 2. I became much more open minded with the use of these tools as far as what I can accomplish with them, adn what I cant. Lab 3: I learned how to show information that actually matters, and some things to avoid when making a study such as cherry picking.

Looking ahead: Lab 1: I feel as if these skills will be used for the remainder of my time coding. I am confident they will be used in every website that I build. Lab 2: I will definately be using Notebook LM to help me learn in the future, maybe even for this very class. Leonardo AI will probaly just be for fun. Lab 3: Creating strong visualizations is helpful at every point in everyones career, especially when presenting data. I am absolutely sure that I will be using these skills in the future.
